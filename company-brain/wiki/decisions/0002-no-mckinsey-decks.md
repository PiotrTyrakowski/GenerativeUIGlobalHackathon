# 0002: No McKinsey Decks For Henrik

## Decision

For BuildPro, proposal material should be pragmatic and collaborative. Avoid polished strategy theater.

## Rationale

Daniel warned that Henrik dislikes McKinsey-style decks. The proposal should show a concrete pilot path and operating logic.

## Consequence

Decks, briefs, and follow-ups should lead with problem, scope, data path, and decision needed.
