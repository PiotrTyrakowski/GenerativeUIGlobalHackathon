# Artur Wala

## Voice Rules

- Direct and specific.
- Founder typed on a phone, not copywriter casual.
- One observed signal, one business implication, one simple next step.
- Avoid over-explaining.

## Banned Phrases

- "I imagine"
- "I'd love to explore"
- "Unlock transformation"
- "AI-powered solution"
- Long em dash-heavy sentences

## Canonical Pattern

1. Noticed a concrete signal.
2. Name the likely operational consequence.
3. Ask a short, low-pressure question.

## Example Fragment Shape

Hook: "Noticed inventory days moved up after the flow-control restructuring."

Pain: "Usually that means dead SKUs show up in finance before supply chain has a clean owner."

CTA: "Curious if you are seeing that in SAP already?"
