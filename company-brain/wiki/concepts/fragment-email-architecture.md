# Fragment Email Architecture

## Decision

Generate outreach as fragments, not full emails.

## Why

Full generated emails tend to converge into identical skeletons. Fragments preserve human assembly and founder voice.

## Fragment Types

- Hook: observed company-specific signal.
- Pain: consequence that connects to the thesis.
- CTA: short question in Jan's voice.

## Quality Checks

- Does the hook cite a concrete signal?
- Is the pain specific enough to avoid generic AI copy?
- Is the CTA short and plausible?
- Does it avoid banned phrases from `wiki/entities/jan-novak.md`?
