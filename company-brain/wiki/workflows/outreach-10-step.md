# Outreach 10-Step Workflow

1. Start with a validated thesis.
2. Define lookalike criteria.
3. Pull a candidate account list.
4. Enrich for ERP, revenue, geography, and inventory signal.
5. Create or update entity pages for strong prospects.
6. Generate hook, pain, and CTA fragments.
7. Check voice against `wiki/entities/jan-novak.md`.
8. Reject banned phrases and generic AI copy.
9. Export approved fragments to the sending tool.
10. Feed campaign results back into `wiki/pipeline/campaign-state.md`.
